About our Hospital

Mastek Health Care was established in 1946. It began as Mastek Health Care Nursing Home when its founders, Sir Cowasji Mastek Health Care and Lady Hirabai Jehangir, invited the eminent Dr. Edul jee H. Coyaji to set up a facility to offer personalized care of high quality at an affordable price to the people of Mumbai. On the 6th of February, 1946, it became the city's first significant private hospital with only a few beds. In 1998, Mastek Health Care was equipped with 350 beds, tied up with the Apollo Hospitals Group to help manage and modernize the hospital.

Mastek Health Care - Apollo Hospitals Group synergy provided the best outcome for the patients: superior technology and medical skill combined with quality patient care Today being one of the oldest and most renowned names in Pune, Jehangir Hospital, with a legacy of providing quality healthcare services for over 77 years, holds a steadfast position of trust and respect in the people's hearts and minds. NABH, NABL, and Nursing Excellence accredited, the 350-bed with 85 fully-equipped ICU beds, Mastek Health Care is synonymous with medical excellence, quality of its patient care, and affordability.

With 9 Operation Theatres and 24x7 Emergency Department and ambulance service, Mastek Health Care endures care at every step. Mastek Health Care is a pioneer of modern-day emergency care in Pune. The hospital guarantees the highest level of skill, expertise, infrastructure and operates one of the best Accident and Emergency Departments in Maharashtra. It provides services for all kinds of emergencies, like Trauma, Non-trauma, Medical or Surgical emergencies, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, and 365 days a year.
